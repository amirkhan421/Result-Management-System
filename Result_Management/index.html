Welcome to Thal University, Bhakkar

"Thal University Bhakkar is a public sector university that provides advanced, intellectual learning that illuminates human minds and broadens the vision of bringing home opportunities and development. Established in February 2022, the university currently has more than 3000 students enrolled in 18 departments. It provides well-equipped classrooms, state-of-the-art IT labs, libraries, hostels, sports grounds, transport facilities, Jamia Mosque, photocopying and thesis binding services, and a canteen for students."

Why Choose TU?

Thal University, Bhakkar offers a unique blend of academic excellence, state-of-the-art facilities, and a supportive learning environment. With a diverse range of programs, experienced faculty, and a commitment to innovation, it provides students with opportunities to excel in their chosen fields. Vice Chancellor's Message

It gives me immense pleasure getting the opportunity to manage an institution in such a remote corner of the country but with such rich cultural values and untapped potentials for success in every field of life. In light of the opportunities and challenges that come hand in hand for running a new institution, I have a dream for Thal University Bhakkar to become a leader in social and economic development while adhering to our established social norms and ethical ideals. This goal can only be achieved via collaboration, synergy, and responsibility-sharing among all stakeholders in the campus, including faculty, administration, and students, as well as outside stakeholders, such as the community comprising local intellectuals, parents, and the general public."
